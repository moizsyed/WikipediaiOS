Official Wikipedia App for iOS.

Official Wikipedia App for iOS. Wikipedia is the free encyclopedia containing more than 32 million articles in 280 languages, and is the most comprehensive and widely used reference work humans have ever compiled. The app has been rewritten from scratch and is better than ever before. Features: * Speed, the app is faster than ever. * Editing both logged in or not. * Recent pages so you never got lost in the depths of Wikipedia. * Save pages for offline reading. * Share articles with others. * Read articles in a different language. * Wikipedia Zero, supporting zero-rated access with participating carriers. The code is 100% open source and community built. If you are a developer and good at Objective-C, then check us out: http://git.wikimedia.org/summary/?r=apps/ios/wikipedia.git
